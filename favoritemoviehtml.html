Plot Summary

This anime is about an orphan boy (Naruto) who goes through hardships to become a successful ninja in the Village Hidden in the Leaves. Naruto seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming Hokage.

Released

Naruto is a Japanese manga written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto, which was released on September 21, 1999, to November 10, 2014. Because of its popularity, it soon became an anime series, which was released on October 3, 2002, and concluded on February 8, 2017. Naruto Shippuden was released on February 15, 2007, to March 23, 2017.

Director & Producer

The anime series Naruto was directed by Hayato Date, written by Katsuyuki Sumisawa and Junki Takegami, and produced by Studio Pierrot and TV Tokyo. Naruto Shippuden was directed by Hayato Date, Masaaki Kumagai, Yasuaki Kurotsu, and Chiaki Kon; it was written by Junki Takegami, Satoru Nishizono, Yasuyuki Suzuki, Yasuaki Kurotsu, and Masanao Akahoshi; Shippuden was produced by Studio Pierrot.

Music

For Naruto, the music was by the Musashi Project and Toshio Masuda. The music for Naruto Shippuden was by Yasuharu Takanashi.
